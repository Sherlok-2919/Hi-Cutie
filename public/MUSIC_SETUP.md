# Music Player Setup

## Add Your Special Song

1. Choose your special song (MP3 format recommended)
2. Rename it to: **our-song.mp3**
3. Place it in the `public` folder (same location as this file)

## Supported Audio Formats:
- .mp3 (recommended)
- .wav
- .ogg
- .m4a

## Current Path:
The music player is looking for: `/our-song.mp3`

## After Adding:
The vinyl record player will play your song when visitors click the play button. The record will spin while the music plays!

## Tips:
- Keep file size reasonable (under 10MB for web performance)
- MP3 format works best for browser compatibility
- The audio will loop automatically when playing
